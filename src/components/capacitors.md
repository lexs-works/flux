# Capacitors

Capacitors store energy in an electric field. Key parameters: capacitance, voltage rating, ESR, temperature stability.

## Common Types

- **Ceramic** — cheap, good for decoupling, X7R/C0G
- **Electrolytic** — high capacitance, polarized, high ESR
- **Tantalum** — stable, polarized, expensive
- **Film** — low distortion, audio applications
