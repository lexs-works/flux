# SMD Hand Soldering

Soldering small surface-mount components requires magnification, good flux, and steady hands.

## Essential Tools

- Microscope or good magnifier (×10–×20)
- Fine tweezers (type 7, curved)
- Flux — liquid or gel, no-clean preferred
- Soldering iron with fine tip (0.5mm or smaller)

## Technique for 0603

1. Apply flux to pads
2. Tin one pad with a tiny amount of solder
3. Place component with tweezers
4. Reflow the tinned pad while holding component
5. Solder the other pad
6. Clean flux residue
