# Thermal Management

Heat is the enemy of electronics. Every 10°C above rated temperature halves the expected lifetime.

## Passive Cooling

- Heatsinks: larger surface area = better dissipation
- Thermal paste: fill microscopic gaps, don't over-apply
- Natural convection: vertical fins work best

## Active Cooling

- Fans: balance airflow vs noise
- Fan curves: slow at idle, ramp under load
- Dust filters: mandatory for long-term reliability
